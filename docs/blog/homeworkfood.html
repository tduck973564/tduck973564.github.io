My Comfort Food

My ultimate comfort food

My ultimate comfort food has to be burgers. I love burgers because they taste so nice and have so much flavour. They are also really filling and make me feel bloated after an especially big one.

The food most popular in lockdown

Pasta, soup and toilet paper, because when there was a lot of panic buying, those were the things that were usually lacking on the shelves and out of stock. I still don't know why people panic bought toilet paper, or even why toilet paper still exists, bidets are so much better (no I don't have one).

Things I want to learn in Food Studies

The first thing is probably how to make pizza, because I really like pizzas. The second thing has to be how to make chips, because I also like chips. The third thing is how to be safe in the kitchen, because a few undesirable things have happened when I have cooked.
